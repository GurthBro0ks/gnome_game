# Sprint 7 5-Tester Checklist

Use a fresh profile. Do not read the repo docs first.

## What To Do

1. Follow the in-game guide from The Burrow.
2. Gather, Expand, enter Loamwake, and clear Rootvine Shelf.
3. Craft and equip the first Fixture.
4. Unlock Greta through Burrow Post.
5. Claim and pull one Lucky Draw.
6. Clear Mudpipe Hollow, defeat The Mudgrip Warden, and reveal Rootrail.
7. Open The Crack and Clique once each.
8. Restart Play Mode and confirm your progress restores.

## What To Notice

- Was the next step clear without outside help?
- Did any button look available but fail unexpectedly?
- Did any page trap you without Back to Burrow?
- Did shell-only pages clearly explain what is not implemented?
- Did save/load restore what you expected?

## Confusion vs Bug

- Confusion: you can continue, but the UI text or ordering did not make sense.
- Bug: you cannot continue, a button does the wrong thing, rewards duplicate, progress disappears, or a page cannot be escaped.

## Intentionally Stubbed

Ignore missing full systems for Rootrail repair, Vault Treasures, The Crack ladder, Clique backend, Great Dispute, networking, chat, invites, monetization, Treasure Week, and Echo Week.

Report friction and bugs with the Sprint 7 bug report template.
