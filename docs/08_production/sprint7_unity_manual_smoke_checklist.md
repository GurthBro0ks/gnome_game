# Sprint 7 Unity Manual Smoke Checklist

Date: 2026-04-21  
Scope: first-playable stabilization pass

## Setup

1. Open Unity, load the project, and enter Play Mode.
2. Use a fresh local profile or clear the previous local save before the first pass.
3. Keep an eye on the visible debug/status areas. They are intentionally present for this prototype.

## Fresh Profile Path

1. Start in The Burrow and confirm the guide text points to Gather.
2. Gather Dewpond and Mushpatch starter output.
3. Expand the Burrow once and confirm Rootmine becomes unlocked.
4. Enter Loamwake from Strata Gate.
5. Clear Rootvine Shelf with the safe route.
6. Return to The Burrow and confirm Greta intro appears on Burrow Post.
7. Open Fixture Workshop, unlock the first Fixture Cap, craft Root-Bitten Shovel Strap, and equip it.
8. Open Burrow Post and complete Greta intro.
9. Confirm Lucky Draw Week appears, claim the free Lucky Draw, and make one pull.
10. Complete Greta follow-up.
11. Re-enter Loamwake, clear Mudpipe Hollow, and confirm The Mudgrip Warden becomes available.
12. Challenge The Mudgrip and confirm Warden defeat, Loamwake Dirt Cap unlock, and Vault shell visibility.
13. Return to Burrow Post and follow Greta to reveal Rootrail.
14. Confirm Rootrail is a shell only and still has back navigation.
15. Confirm The Crack and Clique entries appear after Rootrail reveal.
16. Open The Crack, run one probe, and confirm depth/Crack Coins update.
17. Open Clique, claim the local stipend, and confirm Favor Marks update.
18. Confirm Great Dispute is clearly stub-only and no chat, invites, networking, or shared state appears.

## Save And Navigation

1. Visit every major page and use Back to Burrow:
   - Loamwake
   - Fixture Workshop
   - Vault of Treasures
   - Burrow Post
   - Daily Duties
   - Rootrail
   - Lucky Draw Week
   - The Crack
   - Clique
2. Exit Play Mode, then enter Play Mode again.
3. Confirm the following restore correctly:
   - Burrow level and Rootmine unlock
   - Rootvine/Mudpipe clears
   - equipped first Fixture
   - Greta unlock and Rootrail reveal
   - Lucky Draw pull count
   - Warden defeated and Loamwake Dirt Cap
   - Crack probe state
   - Clique stipend state
   - tutorial guide progress

## Known Issues To Watch

- Some tall pages may still rely on the current temporary layout and can feel cramped.
- Rootrail, Vault, The Crack, and Clique are shells; verify they are clear and stable, not complete systems.
- Lucky Draw Week is prototype-gated through Greta for this build and is intentionally free-path only.
