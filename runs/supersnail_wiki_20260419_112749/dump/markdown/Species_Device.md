1. Dung Beetle Reactor
Four types of materials, *Dung Beetle Coolant*, *Dung Beetle Graphite Rod*, *Dung Beetle Moderators*, and *Dung Beetle Core* can be found within Dung Beetle Chests or by exchanging Emblem cards in the respective Species War. These are available in five different tiers and can be used to build the Dung Beetle Reactor. These materials can be converted into higher or lower tiers at a rate of 5 lower tier materials to 1 higher tier material. Building the Reactor requires ten of each of the three materials in the same tier, and will grant a bonus to the resource collection speed in species war special incidents, and a buff to all minions. After being built, the ability to produce Supply Boxes using Standard Printing Supply will be unlocked. Each instance of producing Supply Boxes for Standard Printing Supply can only be done once.

Each tier gives +1 to All Minion HP, ATK, DEF, and RUSH. <br> Each tier gives Special Incidents in Species War, Resource Collect SPD +2%.
