=Supreme Relic=
The Supreme Relic menu can be found in the bottom left of the shop. It contains a list of relics that can be claimed upon reaching cumulative spending milestones.<br><br>
There are a total of 20 Supreme Relics in game, ranging in "price" from $4 USD to more than $20K USD, with each providing buffs to various game features. Some Supreme Relics also come with additional bonuses, such as the Peacock Quill, Maze Key, and Heaven Wheel Tome, which are also received upon earning the relic.<br><br>

The Supreme Relic "points", found at the bottom of the Supreme Relic menu, show your current progress towards the next relic and can also act as a total expenditure tracker. These points are expressed in US cents and must be divided by 100 in order to track total dollars spent.
