1. INTEL Dealer
In exchange for 1.5k s, randomly offers you either  from the Rift or  from any other area, selected from realms you have visited. Shows up randomly.

1. Peter Jr.
Peter Jr. will occasionally show up and ask for a review of the game. Giving a 5-star review rewards s. If you give a 0-star review 40 times in the same correspondence (total message length 42) will give  +40 fragments.

1. Salesman
The Salesman shows up to offer a random commodity (including relics). Occasionally, if you've been purchasing items from him, he will offer a survey. Rating him 0-stars 3 times rewards one random item from his list of commodities, as well as his soul. Eventually he will show up again to offer new items.
