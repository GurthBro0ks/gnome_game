1. Dance Contests
Obtaining the Dancing Gala INTEL will upgrade each of the Arenas available for Shining Athena to compete in, one at a time. Each Arena corresponds to a certain of the four outfit types and will feature a number of enemies for Athena to compete against. 

[WIKITABLE OMITTED]

Each Arena has 5 normal opponents to defeat and one boss opponent. Each of Athena's outfit pieces will be compared to that of her enemy, and the one with the highest relevant stat will win that round. At least 5 rounds must be won by Athena for the enemy to be defeated. 5 rounds defeated correspond to 1 star, 6 or 7 rounds correspond to 2 stars, and all 8 rounds correspond to 3 stars, or perfect. Once perfectly defeated, the enemy no longer has to be fought on subsequent runs.

Once all enemies are defeated, a boss will appear and must be perfectly defeated for the Arena to be complete. There are, therefore, 18 possible stars to be won from each arena and as you win more stars, you can gain more rewards (corresponding to the arena).
