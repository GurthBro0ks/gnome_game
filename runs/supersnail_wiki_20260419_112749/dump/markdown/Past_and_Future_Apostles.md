1. Travelling to the Past and Future
Travelling to the Past or Future of any Realm requires the Ma'at Tablet and the appropriate Realm unlocked within the Tablets upgrades. Once equipped, travelling to a Realm now has the option to travel to the Past or the Future of that Realm. 

The entire Realm does not need to be explored in order to travel, though it doesn't prevent travelling either. Each Realm's past or future has an additional number of apostles that can be fought and defeated for rewards- most notably Sand of Time and Ma'at Fragments for upgrading the Ma'at Tablet further. 

Excluding Koryeo, all Minions have special stats of *HIT +50* and *Hit Paralysis RES +50%* and all Apostles have special stats of *HIT +80* and *Hit Paralysis RES +80%*. Below are the Apostles and Minions of the Past and Future.
