1. Hitmen
Hitmen are sent daily by the Demon Gods to assassinate the snail. Once the first exploration of the day is completed, the daily Hitman will show up, starting with Hitman No. 1. Once a hitman has been defeated, they will attempt to run away. The snail can choose to give up the fight or intercept them for more rewards. Once a hitman has been fully defeated in both fights, the next hitman will take their place. It's unknown how many total hitmen there are. Hitmen drop a base amount of W-tads and Demon Chests as well as a Lottery Ticket, although certain Relics can raise the reward amount for defeating them. 

As is usual with most snail fights, in order to use RUSH against the opponent, the weakness of the enemy must be identified. For example: for Hitman No. 1 the snail must possess at least 80 CIV in order to identify his weakness and use RUSH against him. 

Certain hitmen will become friends, partners, or unlock further storylines after fully defeating them:
 Hitman No.4: Big Guy Park
 Hitman No.8: Ghost Zadako
 Hitman No.12: Red Doe
 Hitman No.18: Demon Chan-hee
