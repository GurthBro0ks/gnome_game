1. Begin Friendship
To obtain Kim Chi-yum, complete the Koryeo Key Clue, "Perfect Kimchi."

1. ☆☆☆ > ★☆☆
After 3 letters back and forth, Kim Chi-yum will arrive at Home Base and gift Handmade Kimchi ( +200, Kimchi +100). 

1. ★☆☆ > ★★☆
After 3 letters back and forth, Kim Chi-yum will arrive at Home Base and gift the Alchemy Furnace, unlocking the ability to use Wish Coins.

1. ★★☆ > ★★★
After 4 letters back and forth, Kim Chi-yum will move in permanently with her minion, Koryeo Snail. This will also unlock the Minion Room and Chore Dispatch ability.
