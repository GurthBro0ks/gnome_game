1. National Museum
Loan unused Relics to museums to earn medals.

The Museum can be unlocked while playing through Koryeo and after defeating Hitman No. 3. You will be prompted to Modify Hitman No. 3 into  for 10k  to place Relics in Museums.

There is one museum for each of the Realms and each is available after that Realms' final apostle is defeated. After paying enough B-tads, you can add your Relics to the Museum to show them off and earn medals.  

1. How it Works
Each museum has a total of 16 stands: 1 main stand, 3 designated stands, and 12 normal stands. Each stand (other than the main stand) has one out of the 5 AFFCTs and is scored based on how high that particular AFFCT value is on the chosen relic. The main stand requires a Tier 3 or above orange relic (depends on the Museum) and the entire AFFCT score counts. The designated stands only take specific relics relating to that realm, listed below, and the normal stands will take any relic. 

1. Museum Rating
The better the score of your museum, the better the boosts! Replace [Realm] with whichever Museum has this rating. For instance, if your Koryeo Museum had 220 points, then the buff would be "In Koryeo, INTEL gained +5%."

1. Promotions
Increase the museum's yields through promotions! One promotion is available for each National Museum daily, which will increase visitors to boost the yields. Promotions can be obtained through explorations, Chore Dispatch, or purchased with W-Tads (when attempting to use a promotion you do not own).

Each Museum can only have 1 promotion at a time, and each lasts for 24 hours, boosting medal and B-tad production of that museum.
