Unlike many other games, in Super Snail, chests and similar items can not be opened in the direct, traditional sense. Instead, the player must collect a large amount of the same item in order to open them for single reward. For example, collecting 50 Koryeon treasure maps lets you open them to receive one reward. This design model means that players will receive large amounts of these items as they play. Certain collection items will, in addition to their normal contents, always reward additional items, the amount of which may be dependent on the rarity of primary reward. These can either be consumables, like Reagent or Organ Slime, or parts of a bonus collection item that can be opened for greater rewards.

If the player receives a relic through any of the means on this page that they already have, they will instead receive fragments instead dependent on it's rarity: *10* for blue relics, *20* for purple relics, or *40* for orange relics. Players can not receive duplicates of Bionic Parts or Gear (excluding material gear, which can be received indefinitely).

While dissimilar to the other collection items in the collection stockpile, Memetic Catalyst is also included on this page.
