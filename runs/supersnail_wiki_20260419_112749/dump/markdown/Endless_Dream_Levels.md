=Guide=
The goal is to protect the magic lamp.

Every time you rub the lamp, you'll draw dream weapons. Weapons can be dragged up into the grid space to activate them. Combining duplicate weapons upgrades their effects.

Completing a wave rewards Magical Lamp Oil. Magical Lamp Oil can be used to refresh lamp wishes.

Energy bars are located under each hero and indicate when their special skill can be activated.
