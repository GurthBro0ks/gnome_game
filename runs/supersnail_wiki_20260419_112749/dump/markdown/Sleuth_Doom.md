1. Sleuth Doom ♂
A detective from hell. Weak as he is, he is good at lying and controls some demon minions.

1. Begin Friendship
To obtain Sleuth Doom, fully upgrade (level 30) Murika South hero, Sleuth Doom.

1. ☆☆☆ > ★☆☆
After 7 letters back and forth, Sleuth Doom will show up at Home Base and gift 10k B-tads.

1. ★☆☆ > ★★☆
After 8 letters back and forth, Muscle Ran will show up for a fight. After winning the battle and 3 more letters, Sleuth Doom will gift 20k B-tads.
