1. Apostles
Once an apostle is defeated for the first time, he is considered defeated. Once an apostle is defeated in 2 rounds or less, he is considered "Dominated." Each Realm has an apostle and occasionally an apostle will have minions who must be defeated first. Once the apostle is Dominated, there are no more fights with that apostle.

After defeating the last apostle of Cathay, the next Realm, Murika, is unlocked. Below are the stats for every Cathay apostle and their minions.
