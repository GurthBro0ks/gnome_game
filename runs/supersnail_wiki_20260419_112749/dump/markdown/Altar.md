1. Altar
The Divine Altar is a key mechanic in Super Snails. Here you can offer souls, wish to the Divine Dragon, meet gods, and encounter a variety of strange characters. Initially, there are 4 slots where you can offer 1 item in each slot. Each offering gives a certain percentage to a total of 100%, and once 100% is reached a god is summoned. These rewards are listed below. Once the sword is pulled from the stone (see Excalibra), 5 new slots are opened up at the altar for offerings although any 48h offerings cannot be placed here.

1. Offerings and Divine Chests
The types of offerings that can be made are souls, , , and Gaia's organs. Each item has a variety of effects. Most souls are offered to the Pantheon, though some unique items are offered to a specific entity. Unique events and offerings are listed farther below.

1. Souls and Unique Offerings
There are 3 types of souls: Order, Neutral, and Chaos. Yellow souls correspond to Order, blue for Neutral, and red for Chaos. 

There are over 300 different souls that can be acquired, each relating to a defeated enemy. Some are simple enemies encountered during explorations (such as the Cultist in Koryeo) while others can be more complicated such as Agents or Hitmen or Invaders. Overall, each new soul type gives a bonus when offered for the first time.

1. Weekly Offering Event
Every week is one of three Weekly Events, one of which is Offering Week. During this week, the more you offer, the more you get! While the general structure of each Offering Event is the same, the rewards vary from week to week. Specifically, different Event Souls and Relics can be obtained through Earth's Will and Crystal Boom. 

-*This information is accurate for the week of:** October 6 - October 12

1. Suggested Strategies
- Save, save, save! Just like with the other weekly events, make sure you're hoarding all your , , , and your best souls.

-The event also gives extra points for opened Dragon Chests and Divine Order, Neutral, and Chaos Chests. Only open these during Offering Week!

-For your daily tasks, offer the weakest of souls. Specifically, the 2% souls. You can buy these for super cheap from the Vending Machine and hoard all the best 80% souls for the right time.

-Offer all your 48h offerings starting during **Lottery Week**. Yep, that's right! Since they take so long, it's recommended to offer them on the Thursday during Lottery Week so as not to waste all of your hard-earned, hard-saved Offering Speedup. For those who have pulled the sword from the stone (See Excalbara), four 48h items can be offered. For those who have *not* pulled the sword, only three 48h items should be offered so the Daily Tasks can still be completed. Just be careful not to claim them  yet! 

-Hours only count when you claim them! All souls can be offered in advance!

1. Mystic Ritual
1h of Offering = 1 Point. There are 5 rounds total. Each round requires 240 Offering Points and gives 1,100 Spirit Crystals plus other rewards. Completing all five rounds will require 1,200 Offering Hours and awards 5,500 Spirit Crystals.

1. How Many Spirit Crystals Do I Need?
1. 3,000 Spirit Crystal Reward
To get the **3,000 Spirit Crystal Reward** you will need a total of **6,000 Spirit Crystals** to buy the previous rewards. The least amount of offering time you will need to do this is **570 hours**.
- **Complete all of the supplemental rewards:** 2,800 Spirit Crystals
- **Purchase all 7 Offering Bonuses:** 700 Spirit Crystals
- **Complete Two Rounds (240 Offering Hours each):** 2,200 Spirit Crystals
- **90 additional Offering Points:** 300 Spirit Crystals
- **Total:** 6,000 Spirit Crystals [Optionally you can also purchase the 100 W-Tad Event Pack for an additional 100 Spirit Crystals.]

1. Two 3,000 Spirit Crystal Rewards
To get two **3,000 Spirit Crystal Rewards** you will need a total of **9,000 Spirit Crystals.** This can be achieved by completing all five rounds in the Mystic Ritual and acquiring all other sources of Spirit Crystals. Completing five rounds of Mystic Ritual requires **1,200 hours** of offering time.
- **Complete all of the supplemental rewards:** 2,800 Spirit Crystals
- **Purchase all Seven Offering Bonuses:** 700 Spirit Crystals
- **Complete all Five Rounds (240 Offering Hours each):** 5,500 Spirit Crystals
- **Total:** 9,000 Spirit Crystals [Optionally you can also purchase the 100 W-Tad Event Pack for an additional 100 Spirit Crystals.]

1. 5,000 Spirit Crystal Reward
Currently it is not possible to get the **5,000 Spirit Crystal Reward** without spending real money. The most you can earn without spending money is **9,000 Spirit Crystals** which will allow you to buy two **3,000 Spirit Crystal Rewards**

1. Wishes and Rewards
Earth's Will gives some extra wish options during Offering Week, though you can only choose 3! During each event, a special orange Relic and unique soul is chosen by the developer team, so hopefully luck is on your side!

Each wish is accompanied by 1 of the 3 types of Divine Chest +30, until you've gotten each type. Check "Altar Miracles" under your Weekly Event tab to keep track.
