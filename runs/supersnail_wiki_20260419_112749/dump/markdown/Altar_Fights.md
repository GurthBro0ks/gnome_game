1. Excalibra
Upon unlocking the Altar, a sword embedded in a stone can be found at its base. Removing the sword is a prerequisite for accessing most of the Altar fights, including Excalibra. To remove the sword, you need 10,000 of each AFFCT stat. Removing it will reward the player with the  relic, 50k B-tads , 500 W-tads , 5k Wood , and 5 more offering slots. Excalibra will then be released from the stone, tauntingly blocking your way to the Altar's seal. You will not be able to release the darkness that is beneath the Altar without defeating him first.

1. How to Unseal the Altar
The Altar Demon Lords are powerful beings that were sealed beneath the Altar by Earth's Will, unreachable until the sword in the stone is removed and Excalibra is defeated. You must also shoot the Unknown Creature in front of your shrine with  10 times in order to obtain the  relic, as well as shoot down the Whale Warrior when he appears in the sky at your home. Once these requirements are met, the Unknown Creature will be found sleeping at your Altar. Interacting with it will wake it up and cause it to soil the bandages acting as a seal, releasing the first Demon Lord.
