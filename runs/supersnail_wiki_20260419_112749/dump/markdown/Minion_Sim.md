1. Minion Sim
The Minion Sim is a place for you to test and compete with your Minions every week. It's unlocked by purchasing it at Club Shop for 1k Star Coins.

1. Sim Value Packs
Sim Value Packs can be purchased after certain milestones are met.

1. Sim Core
The portion of the Minion Sim that lets you upgrade your Minion Sim.

1. Algorithm Center
This place allows you to upgrade your Minion Sims and spend Log Fragments.
