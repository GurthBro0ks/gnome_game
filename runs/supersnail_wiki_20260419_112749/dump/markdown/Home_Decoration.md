= Home Decorations =
Home Decorations are different than Dorm Decor for your Partners. Home Decorations are a way to decorate your Home Base with skins. You can select these skins by going to the Terminal and clicking on the weather. Home Decorations give Home Comfort which does not seem to have an in game purpose yet.
