1. INTEL
The INTEL Tree in the Time Rift contains many tiles that are extremely Intel-expensive to unlock, and can be upgraded many times over. Some of the later tiles in the tree can be upgraded up to 50 times!

As you travel the Temporal Turbulence, you'll visit alternate Dimensions of the Realms you've already conquered. The Reward Grid in the Time Rift changes its squares depending on which of these Dimensions you're in. Because of this, 8 INTEL Tree tiles upgrade the Reward Grid for their respective Dimensions.

-*Recommended Upgrade:** Before upgrading individual dimensions, prioritize the center lane (1, 3, and 7). Next, upgrading tile 12 to at least level 3 will unlock Purple Card Conversions, and the Time Flute. Combined with gear from the previous Time Rift Cards tiers, you'll be able to use the Eternal Return for +8% DMG and +8% INTEL gained in the Rift.

1. Time Rift INTEL Tree - Page 3
After dominating 30 Time Rift Apostles, Page 3 will unlock. All nodes have no max level. Every 10 levels per Circle will switch to other Circle to level up. For example Outer Circle all nodes are at level 10 they can't be upgraded, instead four nodes of Inner Circle can be upgraded. Once the Inner Circle is fully upgraded (all 4 nodes at 10 Levels), it will switch back to Outer Circle. It keeps switching back and forth.

In the Center, it uses 2 Time Protokaryon per level that are obtainable at Rift II, Rift III and Rift IV by defeating and dominating Time Apostles who are significally stronger than previous Rift Apostles.
