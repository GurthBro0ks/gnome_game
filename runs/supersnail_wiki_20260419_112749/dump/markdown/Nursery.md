1. Minions
Each time a Partner moves into Home Base, they bring with them a unique minion. Each minion has a type equivelant to one of the five main Evolutionary Forms, a set of HARD Stats, Power Level, and sepcial ability in Troop Combat. 

Minions are primarily used for Chores, which take a certain amount of time and yield various rewards. Once the Rocket is finished building, Minions can also be used in Troop Deployment and mining in Domains. Once the Portal is finished building in your Club, Minions can also be deployed to fight and dig in Species War. 

Each Minion type has a set of eight Relics that, when obtained and upgraded, provide buffs to the Minion during combat. Minions can be upgraded and cloned using the Cloning Tank, and healed after battle using the Healing Tank.

1. Cloning Tank
The Cloning Tank can be used to clone and upgrade minions, depending on the level of the tank. At first, only 1 of the 6 tanks is available. After upgrading, new tanks can be used to create a "queue" of minions. **Only one minion type** can be cloned **or** upgraded at one time, however the queue will then upgrade/clone the next minion type as the first finishes. Up to 5 minions can be upgrading/cloning at one time.

1. Healing Tank
The Healing Tank can be used to heal minions that have fallen in troop battles. At first, only 1 of the 6 tanks is available. After upgrading, new tanks can be used to create a "queue" of minions. **Only one minion type** can be healed at one time, however the queue will then heal the next minion type once the first finishes. Up to 10 minions of one type can be healing at one time. 

The healing tank requires healing potion to heal minions, the quantity of which that can be produced depends on the level of your Healing Tank. Healing Potion is produced from food and takes no time to create. 

At first you will only be able to level up the healing tank to Level 9 as the NPWT Technology from the Kemet Domain is required. Once NPWT Technology is acquired, the healing tank maxes out at Level 30.
