1. Whats the best Relic?
There is no Best Relic.<br>Orange SSS have the best overall stats, followed by SS, S, AAA, AA, A, Blue, and Green.<br>Pick from what you need.

1. How do I get other forms? How do I get Dragon Form?
1. Upgrade your base snail! Maxing out those levels unlock different forms. Visit Evolution for more info.
1. Upgrade all other forms to T5 and purchase it in DNA Evolution Module->Growth Track->DNA Bonus->DNA Value Pack

1. When is Daily Reset?
6am PST. Maintenance is 3:30-6:30 am PST every Friday to prepare for a new Event and patch any bugs.

1. Mirror or Lu Bu?
Most players prefer using the Mirror Realm Trait overall, and the Lu Bu Realm Trait for dominating* Apostles.<br>
<sup>*</sup>Defeating in 2 turns or fewer in Koryeo and Yamato, defeating in 3 turns or fewer in Cathay, Murika, and Britland
