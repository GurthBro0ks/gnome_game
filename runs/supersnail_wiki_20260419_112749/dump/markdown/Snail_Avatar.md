1. Basic Info
Once you begin playing, you will have basic access to some Avatars that can be purchsed to boost your stats. Initially, only the "Individual Form Avatar" album is available. Avatars in this album can be obtained (almost) exclusively by raising the Tier of your Forms through Evolution and obtaining Minions.

After Leeku reaches 1 heart of friendship, she will award Makeup Box which, on use, unlocks the rest of the albums. Once available, each album can be obtained by first having the necessary overall Avatar Score and then by purchasing with . Purchasing avatars within each album will raise your Total Avatar Score and the Rating of that particular Album. Each Album provides a different category of buffs, and as the rating improves, so does that category. Once S+ rank is reached for that album, you will obtain a certain unique green relic. Once the Total Avatar Score reaches a certain amount, there are other unique green relics that will be obtained.

Avatars can only be purchased if 
1.The album it is in has been unlocked and activated
1.The character that avatar represents has been defeated by the snail
1.The avatar is purchased with  and /

 **Important Note**: All unlocked and activated avatars always contribute to your total avatar score, rating, and buffs. It doesn't matter what avatar you have currently equipped; the avatar you have equipped is a purely aesthetic choice
