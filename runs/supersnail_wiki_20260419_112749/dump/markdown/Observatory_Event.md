1. Observatory Info
During Offering Week the Observatory Event is available.

1. Each Astrological Divination costs 1 Star Stone.
1. After the divination, you may observe normal Star Pattern or Resplendent Star Pattern; you will receive 10-15 Star Sketches for Normal Star Pattern; and 40-60 Star Sketch for Resplendent Star Pattern.
1. You can claim a reward for every 60K Star Sketches within the cross-server. Player who contributed most receives the Master Astrologer title. There are 8 server(s) attending the event.
1. Every 10 divinations must grant a Resplendent Star Pattern.
1. Star Stone and Star Sketch will not be cleared when the event ends, and you can save them for the next event.
