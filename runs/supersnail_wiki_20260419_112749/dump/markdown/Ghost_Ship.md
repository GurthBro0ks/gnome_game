1. Ghost Ship
The dead is still here!

1. Acquire Fleet Management INTEL tile 8 "Age of Exploration" to unlock the Mysterious Sea

2. Clear 100% of the first 5 seas to obtain 

3. Unlock the Doomsday Sea and activate the Psychic Tower

4. Travel to the top right corner of the floor and defeat the Ghost Ship 

When first obtained in Britland, the Ghost Ship reduces enemy Earth, Water, Fire, and Wind RES by 2, reduces enemy CRIT by 0.4%, and reduces enemy HIT by 0.2%. This is at level 1, and after upgrading to maximum (level 30), the Ghost Ship reduces enemy Earth, Water, Fire, and Wind RES by 100, reduces enemy CRIT by 20%, and reduces enemy HIT by 10%. and can then be unsealed with . Note that while ELMT REDUC applies to all enemies, CRIT and HIT reductions only apply to other players. It is recommended to use this for all PvP battles, specifically the .

1. Upgrade Materials
Upgrading the Ghost Ship requires Sailor's Wail, which is obtained anytime a Sailor dies during exploration of the Mysterious Sea. This is often not enough to fully upgrade the Ghost Ship, so Fleet Management INTEL tile 15 "Sea Burial" is needed to trade NAV coins for Sailor's Wail. They are bought by attempting to upgrade part of the Ghost Ship without enough Sailor's Wails which will give a prompt to buy enough to max that part.

1. Unsealing
Once the Ghost Ship has been upgraded to the max level (30), you have the option to Unseal the Spirit trapped inside. Doing this costs 300 Magic Potion. Unsealing releases the spirit Robinson, who will gift you one of each orange Fungus as a thank you. 

Upgrading Robinson is quite expensive, but doing so gives permanent buffs to your snail.
