1. Cathay Generals
Throughout Cathay, Generals can be appointed to assist you in battle. They can be acquired from INTEL, card conversion, and key clue events. Each general has a skill that applies to the snail and a unique skill that applies to multiple generals or the snail. Upgrading Generals requires , which is gained during normal exploration and can be improved with INTEL.

Generals will die in battle, and be revived in the next exploration, or rarely with 7-Star Lamp once unlocked.

If the exploration is sped up, the **generals will not die to random encounters along the way.**
