= Temporal Drifters =
Temporal Drifters are supporting characters you can unlock in the Time Rift. You can level them up in the Time Beacon. 

Temporal Drifters all use  to level up along with one or two other components. The sets of components can be used on two or three Temporal Drifters. Each set of components enhances the same set of Temporal Drifters, drop consistently in the same regions, and are similar to enhancement items from previous Realms. Each set of components drops from five of the forty zones in Time Rift.

1. NAV Medal and Nautical Treasure
 and  are used to enhance Peacekeeper, St. Trinidad, and Soleil Royal. They drop in zones **1, 4, 17, 19, and 24** of Time Rift.

1. Demon Leather and Color Thread
 and  are used to enhance Athena and Dracula. They drop in zones **2, 20, 25, 35, and 37** of Time Rift.

1. Hitman Cert and Apostle Cert
 and  are used to enhance Hitman No. 1, Idol Boy, and Koryeon Commissioner. They drop in zones **3, 8, 9, 29, and 34** of Time Rift.

1. Adv Taoist Pill and Adv Herb Pill
 and  are used to enhance Naga the Dragon Horse and Sanzang the Merti Buddha. They drop in zones **5, 6, 23, 33, and 40** of Time Rift.

1. Ninjutsu Cert and Sword Expert Cert
 and  are used to enhance Excalibra, Ninja Amakusa, and Ninja Momochi. They drop in zones **7, 12, 16, 22, and 28** of Time Rift.

1. Super Energy Stone and Super Obsidian
 and  are used to enhance Future Pyramid and Past Pyramid. They drop in zones **10, 11, 21, 31, and 39** of Time Rift.

1. Mecha Component+ and Biochem Component+
 and  are used to enhance Fishing Master, Mad Abe, and Night Kitty. They drop in zones **13, 15, 18, 27, and 36** of Time Rift.

1. Tiger Tally (Yang) and Tiger Tally (Yin)
 and  are used to enhance Demonic Guan Yu, Madam Huang Ex, and Poisoner Hua Tuo. They drop in zones **14, 26, 31, 32, and 38** of Time Rift.

= Farming Strategies =
If you do an entire run of Time Rift you'll go through all forty zones which will give five zones that drop each set of components. If you want to farm a specific component set, it's best to just use s to rush to the zones that drop the components you want. But how far should you go? Which zone is the most efficient to stop in to save s? That is what these guides will tell you.
