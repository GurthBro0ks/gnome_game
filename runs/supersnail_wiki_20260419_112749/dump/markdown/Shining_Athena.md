1. Shining Athena
To obtain Hellas' unique element, first defeat the 1st Apostle of Hellas, the Acting Manager of Aries. He will drop a Pot of Sealing which, when used, unseals the Goddess Athena from within. 

Shining Athena is a dress-up game, where you obtain *Leather*, *Crystals*, *Feathers*, and *Thread* to spin for individual pieces of outfits. Once Athena is dressed, she can compete in Dancing Galas for rewards. Each arena focuses on one of the four types: Posh, Moody, Cute, and Wicked.

1. Goddess' Bond
Obtaining the Mental Bond INTEL unlocks the ability to raise favorability with Athena, thus increasing your favorability with her. Initially, only 10 points per day are possible but as you increase the INTEL level of Oath of Heart you can receieve up to 50 points per day. Tap on Athena up to 10 times per real-life day to increase favorability. Achieving a certain level of Bond with Athena rewards certain items.

1. Outfits
Athena's outfit is divided into eight total parts: Headwear, Necklace, Top, Bottom, Socks, Hand, Bag, and Shoes. In addition to this, each part will be predominant in one stat (Posh, Moody, Cute, or Wicked) and be either green, blue, purple, or orange rank. In total, this is 160 possible outfit permutations.

1. Upgrading
Upgrading outfits requires individual outfit pieces, which are collected through the Costume Star Chart, which is a random lottery. You can draw 1x or 10x of Leather, Crystal, Feather, or Thread to randomly obtain a number of fragments for an associated outfit piece. A certain number of fragments are required to unlock a piece (T0 to T1), and to upgrade it further. Green items can only go to T3, blue items to T4, purple items to T5, and orange items to T6. Once an item is fully upgraded, fragments for that piece that are later obtained are converted into Costume Coins

1. Obtaining Outfit Items
The only two ways to obtain outfit items is through either the Constellation gacha mechanic or through the shop.

1. Costume Star Chart
The Costume Star Chart is the gacha mechanic used to obtain outfit items for Athena, split between the four different outfit currencies: Leather, Crystal, Feather, and Thread. Each currency has its own roll chances and items, costing 100 for 1 draw or 1,000 for 10 draws. Non-outfit items can only be obtained once before being removed from the pool.

1. Costume Shop
The Costume Shop is unlocked through the Olympus Market INTEL. The shop allows you to spend accumulated Costume Coin to buy specific outfit items rather than roll for them randomly with the Costume Star Chart.

Costume Coin is accumulated anytime you roll an outfit item fro the Costume Star Chart that you have already fully upgraded/ascended. Every fragment will be converted into 10 Costume Coin. Once an item has been fully upgraded/ascended, it is no longer available to buy from the shop.
 10  Fragments cost 200 Costume Coin
 20  Fragments cost 700 Costume Coin
 40  cost 2,500 Costume Coin
 60  cost 6,000 Costume Coin

1. Skills
Athena's outfits unlock various skills when wearing items of the same rank and the same type on all four ranks or all eight types. When you wear all items from a single outfit, you gain an additional bonus skill. Some of these skills will boost HARD or ELMT Stats or immediately reward items.
