1. INTEL
There are three INTEL trees in Kemet, each with 19 different skills. Each skill has between 1 and 3 levels. Initially, only one hex is available to upgrade in exchange for INTEL (gathered passively during exploration). To make any hex available, an adjacent hex must first be fully upgraded. Additionally, some hexes have a "release condition" required before it can be unlocked. Below is a list of all Kemet INTEL.

-*Recommended Upgrades:** Tiles 5, 6, 8, and 9 for each INTEL increase the output of the Quarry Grids. Obtaining these faster will reduce the necessary explorations to fully upgrade the Pyramids.  Tiles 10 and 13 will increase the DMG of the Pyramid of whichever time is being explored while tile 11 increase the Durability. Tile 14 for the Present and Future should be obtained as soon as possible for the module, and Kemetic INTEL tile 19 for the gear.
