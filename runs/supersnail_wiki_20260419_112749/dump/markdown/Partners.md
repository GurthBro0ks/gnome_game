1. Partners
Partners are gained from a variety of places. Once encountered, they'll begin a friendship storyline that involves sending letters back and forth, advising them on the best path forward in their troubles (and occasionally fighting others for them), and sending and receiving gifts until their friendship level reaches 3 hearts. 

Once a partner is at 3 hearts they will move into a room with their unique minions. These minions can be used during Chore Dispatch and can fight in the Divinity Domains in teams. Check out minions and Domains for more info. Each partner has a Gene Specialization, which makes them more efficient at helping to research that specific gene. Their minion will have the same Gene Type which relates to various skills. For instance, some relics boost Troop stats based on how many of a certain minion type there are in troop formation. 

Partners also have two skills, the second of which is the Commander skill. This skill is acquired in the Koryeo Domain via the  and increases the amount of a certain minion type that can be cloned and increases Troop stats per 10 of their own minions in a troop formation. The first skill is unique to each partner and typically increases one of Snail AFFCT stat and a home resource or gene speedup. Each partner is listed below with their first skill and minion. Click each name to learn more about that partner.
