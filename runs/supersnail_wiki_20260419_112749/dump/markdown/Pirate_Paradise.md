1. Pirate Paradise
Pirate Paradise is the 1st map within the Mysterious Sea. Unlock Pirate Paradise by obtaining Fleet Management INTEL tile 8.

Places are determined with rows and columns. Therefore, C1R4 means the 1st column and 4th row, C5R2 means the 5th column and 2nd row, and so on.
