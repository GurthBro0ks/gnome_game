1. Incense

Incense is a special mechanic allowing for multiple unique events, rewards, and storylines. First, you need to access the Incense Shrine, also called the Buddha Altar. To get there, go to the Gene Room and tap the button on the wall. It allows you to switch between the Arsenal, where you can view your gear, and the Shrine, where you can use Incense .

3 incenses can be loaded at a time, with each incense being subdivided into 5 charges. This is extremely important info later on for making the most of your incense.

1. What to Use Incense On
From time to time, critters will show up in the Gene Room. The most common are rats, frogs, and cockroaches. Tapping on the animal while having at least one incense charge left in the altar will fire a laser at the critter.
Firing at them will reduce them to ashes, giving 200 Demon God Cells. Frogs give Zombie Cells , Roaches give Mutant Cells , and Mice give Demon Cells , Angel Cells , and Mecha Cells .

Occasionally, other critters will spawn besides the usual frog, roach, or mouse. Shooting them may trigger unique events or rewards. *More detail under Special Incense Events.*

Loading 3 incense into the shrine and tapping it repeatedly will make the shrine angry and shoot at the sky. An object that can be collected for rewards will fall into the pond. Reaching certain milestones may also trigger unique events, storylines, and rewards. *More detail under Shooting the Sky.*

1. How to Get Incense
Incense is difficult to get reliably, and is mostly obtained through luck. The Vending Machine has some incense packs which can be purchased once, but most other known ways of getting incense involve chance.

1. Shooting the Sky
Finally, loading 3 incense and tapping the altar repeatedly will "make the shrine angry" and cause him to shoot at the sky. Note that this will consume all of your incense, *however* only 2 and 1/5 stick is truly needed: as long as you have one tick of durability left on the third stick, you can shoot the sky, meaning you can take 4 shots at other critters and still be able to shoot the sky. Shooting down 5 critters, however, will not leave you with enough incense, so be careful!

There are two types of events that may occur when shooting the sky. The first and most common type is shooting down a helicopter.
