1. Clubs
Clubs are a core multiplayer mechanic within Super Snails. After defeating the 8th of the Koryeon Apostles, Gvent Guru, a portal appears allowing you to choose a club to join, or the option to create a club yourself.

1. Club Creation
Creating a Club requires the Registration of a Club Name, with an 18 alphanumeric character limit, and a Short Name, with a  3-5 alphanumeric character limit. This costs 1k W-Tads to do.

1. Joining a Club
To join a club, either search for a known club name within your server, or browse a list of clubs available to join. You'll be able to see the Full and Short name of the club as well as their level, current President, and collective power and members. The clubs applicant requirements will also be shown: required power, minion power, and leadership as well as if a club review is necessary before joining. 

-Things to Note: There's a 24h cooldown on participating in Species War events after joining a new club.*

Joining a club is completely free. 

1. Switching Clubs
Switching clubs is very easy; simply go to Manage and Quit Club, then start the process over. 

-Things to Note: There's a required 30s cooldown before joining a new club, and a 24h cooldown on Species War participation.*

1. Star Coins
Every club member can claim 200 Star Coins each day for free (as well as 10 Club Chests and 20 Club EXP). After the initial free claim, you can spend W-Tads to purchase additional Star Coins, Club Chests and B-Tads; the limit on this is 4 times per day after the free daily claim.

Star Coins can be spend within the Club Shop, which refreshes every 72 hours and is on the same timer as the Arena Shop, Fissure Shop, and Museum Shop.
