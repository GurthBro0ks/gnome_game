= Yamato Apostles =

1. Apostles
Once an apostle is defeated for the first time, he is considered defeated. Once an apostle is defeated in 2 rounds or less, he is considered "Dominated." Each area has an apostle and occasionally an apostle will have minions who must be defeated first. Once the apostle is Dominated, there are no more fights with that apostle. 

After defeating the last apostle of Yamato, the next area, Cathay, is unlocked. Below are the stats for every Yamato apostle and their minions.
