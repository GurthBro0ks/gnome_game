1. General Info
Home Resources are the assets you can gather from The Pond. The Pond is also known as The Courtyard or the Home Facility in the game. These resources are B-Tads, Fungus, Travel Speedups, Wood, and Stone. 

The amount you collect from tapping the Courtyard is dependent on the levels of the buildings in the Courtyard, the relics you have slotted into them, your relics in your possession, and many other factors. There are many factors that change the amounts of the daily output, the max capacity, and the special power besides just the level. As such, those amounts will not be shown in the building level tables, only the costs and the amount of relic slots will be noted.

Each building in the Courtyard has a special power as well. 

Getting 30 Relic Stars is what unlocks the ability to click on the wooden sign, upgrade buildings, slot one relic into buildings, and have the option to buy the Lumber Camp and the Quarry.

1. Upgrade Costs Calculations
There are a lot of building discounts that lower the costs of buildings. In later game, you'll not see a single cost at it's actual base cost. 

Since the costs don't mean anything to you without you knowing what discounts I have, I'm reverse engineering the costs to a close approximation of their original value. All values in the tables below are as close to their base value as I can approximate. There are many rounding issues and it's hard to get an exact value for each building level. 

Fortunately, the calculations for each buildings level costs are consistent across every building at the same level. Each building needs the same amount of total materials (stone + wood), B-Tads, and Relic Stars for each level. The difference is the distribution per building type of materials. Each building has a different percentage of stone and wood they need from the total amount of materials for each level.

1. Relic Slots
Building all have Relic Slots in them that allow you to place a Relic in the building to enhance it's production. All buildings Unlock the first slot at Level 1, the second slot at Level 15, and the third and final slot at Level 35.

1. Snail Avatars
The Mirage I Avatar category is completely about Home Resources and their buildings. Leveling up this Avatar category will give discounts to building costs and increase production of buildings.
