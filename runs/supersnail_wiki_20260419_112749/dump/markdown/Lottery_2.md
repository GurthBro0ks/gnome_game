1. Understanding Relics
Relics each have FAME, ART, FTH, CIV, and TECH stats (abbreviated to AFFCT), one of which is higher and the dominant trait for each relic, and this is how they are predominately sorted.

Activating relics requires B-tads, and upgrading them requires reagent and fragments. The buffs and stats that a relic gives are always active, regardless of where or if it's embedded in the compass, or elsewhere. At Tier 3 and higher, skills are unlocked as well as mirage fights for orange relics. Once fully upgraded, relics can be awakened to contribute to resonance buffs. Some relics can only be obtained in the Premium shop for USD or CAD and are known as Supreme Relics. 

For a more detailed view of everything relics, see the Relic Guide.
