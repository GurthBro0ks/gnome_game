1. INTEL
There are two INTEL trees in Bharata, each with 19 different skills. Each skill has between 1 and 4 levels. Initially, only one hex is available to upgrade in exchange for INTEL (gathered passively during exploration). To make any hex available, an adjacent hex must first be fully upgraded. Additionally, some hexes have a "release condition" required before it can be unlocked. Below is a list of all Bharata INTEL.

-*Recommended Upgrades:**
- Bharata INTEL Tree

-* 6 - to improve the drops
-* 9 - at least the first upgrade to unlock the All-In-One tree

-* 1 - to reach 2 and 4
-* 2 - to upgrade Medicine Pavilion
-* 4 - to upgrade Rock Pavilion
