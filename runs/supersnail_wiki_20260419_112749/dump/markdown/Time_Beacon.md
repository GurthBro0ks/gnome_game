=Time Beacon=
The Time Beacon is a unique element to the Time Rift. It contains your Temporal Drifters and Anecdotes.

You unlock the Time Beacon when you Defeat 4th Apostle (Dawie Jones - AD 1556 - Berfast) and unlock St. Trinidad and Soleil Royal.

=Temporal Drifters=
Drifters are recruited as you defeat apostles in the Time Rift. Each Drifter will have a Rift exploration bonus, 5 skills for Anecdote trials, and Talents. When a drifter is recruited, they are at level 1 with 1 skill unlocked. Drifters are leveled up by spending Time Fruit and additional items collected while exploring the rift. Skills are learned by having Drifters attend the skill Academies in the Time Beacon. Skills are leveled by spending Time Worms.

=Anecdotes=
Anecdotes are a series of challenges in the Time Beacon in the Time Rift. When the Time Beacon is unlocked, the only Anecdote that is available is Temporal Barrier - 01. When you defeat an Anecdote you gain rewards and unlock further challenges.

1. Academy
Academy events teach select Temporal Drifters certain skills. It takes 12 Hours for a Temporal Drifter to learn a skill. Each Academy can be used multiple times to teach multiple Temporal Drifters. Temporal Drifters have a preset list of skills they can learn and a particular order they have to learn them in. But only one Drifter can be taught at an academy at a time and a Drifter can only be learning one skill at a time. After a Drifter finishes learning a skill, depending on their skill set, they can then go back to the same Academy or a different Academy if they still have open skills. If the Academy is then free, a different student can attend.

The Band Academy is set in AD 1875 and is unlocked by defeating the Paris Opera House Band event. It teaches the following Band skills.
