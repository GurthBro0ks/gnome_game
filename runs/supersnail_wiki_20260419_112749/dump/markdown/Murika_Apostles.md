1. Apostles
Once an apostle is defeated for the first time, he is considered defeated. Once an apostle is defeated in 3 rounds or less, he is considered "Dominated." Each area has an apostle and occasionally an apostle will have minions who must be defeated first. Once the apostle is Dominated, there are no more fights with that apostle.

After defeating the last apostle of Murika, the next Realm, Britland, is unlocked. Below are the stats for every Murikan apostle and their minions. 

A special feature of this Realm is that alongside being split into individual areas like all Realms, it is also split into the North and South. Therefore, every area has two apostles that must be defeated.
