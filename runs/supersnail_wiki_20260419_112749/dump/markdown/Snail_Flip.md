This Minigame is acquired from getting RNG <100 in the Lottery.

Rules:

- Tap on a square to change the color of the blocks around it to its color.
- There are 50 stages in total.
- To progress to the next stage, make all squares one color within a certain number of moves.
- Many puzzles have multiple solutions, but only one will be listed here.

Reading the solution:

- Columns are referenced alphabetically: A, B, C, D, E
- Rows are referenced numerically: 1, 2, 3, 5, 5
