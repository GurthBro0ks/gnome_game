1. Mysterious Sea
The Mysterious Sea can be explored in Britland with the fleet. Once blueprints are obtained, various ships can be built to explore a section of the Mysterious Sea to fight enemies and gain prizes. First, Sailors must be recruited for 10  per sailor. Sailors act as the fleets HP. Only one Sea may be explored per Britland exploration. Once inside a Sea, the fleet cannot be altered and more sailors cannot be recruited.

Each floor follows the same layout: a 6x6 grid with one revealed starting square and filled with "mist." The mist can be cleared for bronze, silver, or gold coins, depending on the Sea, in order to reveal enemies. Once the Boss is cleared and the Whirlpool is found, the fleet can move on to the next floor. The fleet cannot go back to previous floors once it enters the Whirlpool. Once all floors are completely cleared, the Sea is resolved. 

When an enemy or boss appears, the mist in the 8 squares around that enemy cannot be cleared until the enemy is defeated. As an exception, this does not apply when enemies force themselves to appear once the fleet enters a new floor. 

After the fleet battles an enemy, the total sailors is reduced by HP lost. Once the Sailors reach 0, the fleet can no longer battle. However, mist can still be cleared for coins. Once a new adventure is started, new sailors can be recruited and the floor resumed.

1. Doomsday Sea
The final Sea is unlocked after activating the . This is a unique floor where the boss is the Kraken and each enemy is a Tentacle that revives at the next exploration. The Psychic Tower can be activated to assist the fleet, which must consist of the three strongest ships each at max level. 

To complete this Sea, the Psychic Tower needs to first be activated. After activating, four more towers will appear throughout the map. A fleet consisting of the three strongest ships each at max level will only be able to defeat 3 tentacles per exploration. However, once the Psychic Tower is activated, Tentacles can no longer revive and are permanently defeated. Each tower affects the two tentacles surrounding it
