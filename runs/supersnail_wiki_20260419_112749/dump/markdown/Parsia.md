1. Parsia
Camel bells echo on the Silk Road, blades flash by night, and sacred fire ends a thousand tales.

Parsia is the 10th exploration zone, accessible after breaking all three seals on "The Romances of Lord Cao".

1. How To Unlock
1. Dominate Minister Cao Cao in Cathay to get the item "The Romances of Lord Cao".
1. Go to "Choose a Destination" and open the book from there.
1. Go to the Fissure and talk to Miss Crawfer. Pass her tests:
1. Relic Points must be at least 300,000.
1. Display 3-star Relic Soul Stone.
1. Defeat Miss Crawfer in a fight and acquire Talismanic Hoof.
