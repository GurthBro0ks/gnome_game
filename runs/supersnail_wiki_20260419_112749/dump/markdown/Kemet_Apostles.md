1. Apostles
Once an apostle is beaten in combat for the first time, they are considered defeated. Once an apostle has been defeated in 3 rounds or less, they are considered "Dominated" and will no longer have to be fought on subsequent explorations. Each Area has an apostle and occasionally an apostle will have minions who must be defeated first. 

Similar to the Apostles in Murika, Apostles of this Realm are broken up into different areas that can only be explored one at a time. For Kemet you can explore the Present, Past, and Future to encounter all the Apostles. Below are all the Apostles for each area of Kemet.

 Note that **all** apostles have the special stats of: *HIT +70 and Paralysis RES +70%* while **all** minions have the special stats of: *HIT +50 and Paralysis RES +50%*.

After defeating the last apostles of Kemet, the next Realm, Hellas, is unlocked. Below are the stats for every Kemetic apostle and their minions.

See Past and Future Apostles for the Apostles in Kemet Past and Kemet Future.
