When the House of Anjou was established, the King used this badge as their emblem. The name of the dynasty is taken from the nickname of Henry II's father, Count Geoffrey V. It is said that Geoffrey had Plantagenets on his hat. It is customary for royals to make family emblems and the House of Anjou used small branches of Plantagenets to design the family emblem. Hence, it is also commonly known as the House of Plantagenet.
