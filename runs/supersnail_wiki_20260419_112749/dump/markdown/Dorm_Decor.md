= Decor Rating = 
Dorm Decor are special items that provide various bonuses to for your minions.  Each piece of dorm decor increases your Dorm Decor Rating by +10.  There are general bonuses for reaching Dorm Decor Rating milestones, as well as bonuses specific to each minion whose dorm you decorate.  

Dorm Decor can be purchased using s in the Decor Shop.  Dorm decor is often first introduced during special events such as Halloween, Christmas, etc.  These "exclusive" decors are often (if not always?) added to the decor shop a few months after the event.

1. Increasing Dorm Decor Rating
In addition to the +10 you get with each piece of dorm decor owned, you also get a +60 decor bonus from completing a set of 4 decorations for any partner dorm.  This bonus is an excellent way to boost your overall decor, as you spend a total of 1200 decor coins for +100 decor rating.  Having all 4 of a partner's decor also provides a bonus to one of the Snail ELMT damages (see each partner's page for which element the 4th decor bonus provides).

1. Partner Bonues
As you unlock more decor for each partner, you will unlock useful bonuses for your Snail, as well as things like gene research speed and decor rating bonuses.

1. Strategies for spending Dorm Decor Coins
1. Fully Focus on Dorm Decor Rating
If you're focus is strictly on increasing your Dorm Decor rating, the most "bang for your buck" on dorm decor comes from Green Decor.  Green decor costs 100 s, meaning it gives 1 decor rating per 10 coins.  Blue costs 200, Purple costs 350, and Orange costs 550.  If you complete a full set (total cost of 1200 s), then you get the +60 bonus, meaning a full set equates to 1 decor rating per 12 coins.  This means a good strategy is to buy all the Green decor, then focus completing the decor for one room at a time.  If you have 2 or 3 pieces of a single dorm's decor, it is always most cost-effective to complete that dorm's decor to increase dorm decor rating rather than do something like complete buying all the Blue decor.

<strong>Why focus on Decor Rating?</strong>

: Reaching certain overall decor rating milestones can provide useful bonuses to thing like Leadership, Snail HARD STATS (percent bonuses), Snail ELMT DMG (percent bonuses), and Minion HARD Stats.

1. Focus on Partner Research Ability
To speed up your Gene Research, you may want to focus on getting the 3rd dorm decor bonus for some or all of your partners.  This will provide an additional 10 to the partner's research ability in their Gene of specialization.

For Example: The Partner "Kim Chi-yum" provides an Angel type minion.  So having 3 pieces of Kim Chi-yum's dorm decor will give Kim Chi-yum a +10 to Angel Gene Research.

<strong>Why focus on Research Ability?</strong>

: Increasing partner research ability increases your overall Gene Research speed for the partner's form type (ex: Zombie, Mecha, etc.).  Since Gene Research speed is one of the big limiting factors in the game in terms of progression, it can be incredibly useful to balance dorm decor bonuses with these Gene Research bonuses.

1. Dorm Decor Rating Bonuses
These bonuses can be found by going to you dorm, clicking on the ringing phone, clicking "Dorm Decor", clicking on the woman behind the desk, clicking "Overview" at the bottom of the screen, then finally clicking "Decor Bonus" at the bottom of the screen.
