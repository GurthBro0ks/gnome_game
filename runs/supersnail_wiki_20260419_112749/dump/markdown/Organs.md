Organs are a set of items that can be obtained through various sources and provide permanent buffs, in the same way as relics. Once obtained and transplanted, organs will always be active. There are no restrictions to the number of organs that a player can have. All the organs that have been obtained can be viewed by tapping the Gene button in the stats page and selecting the origin form. As green organs are transplanted, the origin form will visually change.

Green organs are obtained naturally as the player evolves the initial Devouring/Muscle/Brian/Vision/Neuron parts. It is impossible to obtain multiple copies of these. Other organs can be obtained through , the weekly feast, and many other places. The sources for each organ are listed on their individual pages.

1. Evolving Organs
Once an organ is transplanted, additional copies of it can be used to increase the stats and effect of the organ. The maximum grade differs based on organ rarity, and evolving to grades higher than 10 costs multiple copies of the organ per evolution. These evolutions cost both black tadpoles and organ slime.<br>*Green Organs can not be evolved.*

[WIKITABLE OMITTED]

1. Organ Recycling
After fully evolving the Devouring, Muscle, Brian, Vision, and Neuron Parts, the ability to recycle extra organs will be unlocked. This can be done using the device on the top right of the origin form's page (the same page where transplanted organs are shown). Once an organ is raised to the maximum tier, any additional copies of that organ can be recycled into organ slime.
