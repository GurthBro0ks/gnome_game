1. Lottery Machine

The Lottery Machine is located in the Gene Room and is a key gacha mechanic in the game. Here you use your Lottery Tickets to spin the machine, and each colored bead (White, Green, Blue, Purple, Orange, Red) provides a reward. You can use 1 Lottery Ticket for one draw, 10 Lottery Tickets for 10 draws, or 3,000 W-Tads for 10 draws.

1. Lottery Tickets
Lottery Tickets come from a variety of places. Get tickets by defeating daily Hitmen, Arena shop, sign-in to the Relics Calendar on Sunday, participate in Weekly Events, purchase Event Bonus from the Vending Machine, collect Chat packs, and from the  Fissure Shop.

1. Lottery Value Packs
Lottery Value Packs can be purchased after certain milestones are met.

1. Weekly Lottery Event
Every week is one of three Weekly Events, one of which is Lottery Week. During this week, the more you draw, the more you get! While the general structure of each Lottery Event is the same, the rewards vary from week to week. Specifically, different Orange Relics award higher points when obtained, and different Relics can be purchased in Lucky Voucher and Balloon Battle.

-*This information is accurate for the week of:** Nov 3-Nov 9

1. Suggested Strategies
-Save, save, save! Just like with the other weekly events, make sure you're hoarding all your Lottery Tickets and Lucky Vouchers. Also consider stashing away a certain amount of W-Tads and Scrolls.
-The event also gives extra points Novice Scrolls, Expert Scrolls, Master Scrolls, Grandmaster Scrolls, and using W-Tads.
-Keep buying those tickets! Check out the Event Bonus in the Vending Machine and purchase Lottery Tickets from the Vending Machine, Fissure, and Arena.

1. Giga Ball
1 Lottery Draw = 1 Point. There are 5 rounds total. Each round requires 500 Lottery Tickets and gives 1,100 Carnival Balloons plus other rewards. Completing all five rounds will require 2,500 Lottery Tickets and awards 5,500 Carnival Balloons.

1. How Many Carnival Balloons Do I Need?
1. 3,000 Carnival Balloon Reward
To get the **3,000 Carnival Balloon Reward** you will need a total of **6,000 Carnival Balloons** to buy the previous rewards. The least amount of Lottery Tickets you will need to do this is **1,060 Lottery Tickets**.
- **Complete all of the supplemental rewards:** 2,800 Carnival Balloons
- **Purchase all 7 Offering Bonuses:** 700 Carnival Balloons
- **Purchase 100 W-Tad Event Pack:** 100 Carnival Balloons
- **Complete Two Rounds (500 Lottery Tickets each):** 2,200 Carnival Balloons
- **60 additional Lottery Points:** 240 Carnival Balloons
- **Total:** 6,040 Carnival Balloons

1. Two 3,000 Carnival Balloon Rewards
To get two **3,000 Carnival Balloon Rewards** you will need a total of **9,000 Carnival Balloons.** This can be achieved by completing all five rounds in the Giga Ball and acquiring all other sources of Carnival Balloon. Completing five rounds of Giga Ball requires **2,500 Lottery Tickets.**
- **Complete all of the supplemental rewards:** 2,800 Carnival Balloons
- **Purchase all Seven Offering Bonuses:** 700 Carnival Balloons
- **Complete all Five Rounds (500 Lottery Tickets each):** 5,500 Carnival Balloons
- **Total:** 9,000 Carnival Balloons [Optionally you can also purchase the 100 W-Tad Event Pack for an additional 100 Carnival Balloons.]
