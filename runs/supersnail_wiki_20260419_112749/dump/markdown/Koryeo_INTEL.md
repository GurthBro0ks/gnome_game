There are two INTEL trees in Koryeo, each with 19 different skills. Each skill has between 1 and 3 levels. Initially, only one hex is available to upgrade in exchange for INTEL (gathered passively during exploration). To make any hex available, at least one of the adjacent hexes must first be fully upgraded. Additionally, some hexes have a "release condition" required before it can be unlocked.
