1. Murikan Heroes
The recruitment of Heroes is the unique element in Murika and allows you to use the abilities of certain apostles during your exploration throughout the Realm. Once an apostle from the North or South is defeated, they can be recruited to your team. There are 10 total Apostles/Heroes in each Realm, so 20 total that need to be defeated and recruited. Each Hero has one ability and, after acquiring certain INTEL, 3 heroes can be taken on your exploration at once. The relevant INTEL tiles are Mecha INTEL tile 4, lv.3 and Mecha INTEL tile 12, lv.4, so these should be upgraded as soon as possible.

Of the 3 heroes, 1 is the Marshal, and 2 are Adjutants. The Marshal boosts your own stats, but the Adjutants only contribute their skills. Northern Adjutants can only ride with a Northern Marshal, and the same is true of the Southern Adjutants, but a Northern Marshal or Southern Marshal can be chosen despite whichever is being explored. Here we will talk about Southern Heroes, and Murika North Heroes talks about the Northern Heroes.

1. Upgrades
To upgrade each hero requires different Components, which can be picked up during exploration. Of the four Labs, there are two in each region; the Northern components upgrade Northern Heroes and Southern components upgrade Southern Heroes. Once the Hero reaches level 10, 15, 20, 25, and 30 you will receive 200 honor points, which is used to upgrade Lady Liberty.

1. Abilities
Every hero has an ability and a set of four stats: logistics, assault, generalship, and marching. These stats each correspond to a HARD stat and are considered in addition to the snail's own HARD stats during a fight. So considering both the stats and the skills will be important in choosing your formation.
 
1 Logistics = 250 HP
1 Assault = 25 ATK
1 Generalship = 25 DEF
1 Marching = 25 RUSH

This is very unique as, usually, other snails are the only characters that can have RUSH. This makes them very useful for your own explorations. Below is a list of all Heroes and their stats and skills at Lv.30. 

1. Recommendations
A great first formation while exploring the area is Blink Stewart as your Marshal for a faster exploration and Sleuth Doom and Night Kitty as Adjutants for extra Demon God Cells and Reagent. Consider also The Whole Circus and General Lee as potential Adjutants. After your initial exploration and while conquering the realm, more fight-aligned heroes are needed. Consider Dominator as a Marshal and Amazon and Master Archer as Adjutants. Of course, experiment with formations to see what works for you and keep in mind that, generally speaking, ATK > RUSH > DEF > HP. Just as with the North, the Heroes' skills contribute to the snail's stats and only the Marshal's stats will be contributed.
