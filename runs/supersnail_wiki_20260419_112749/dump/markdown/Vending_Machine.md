1. The Vending Machine
The Vending Machine at Home Base near the Mailbox. To unlock it, you must first complete the Tutorial and purchase the  from Koryeo. This greasy old motor has a production batch number engraved: CyberVendor XCC-II-0523. Use this to repair the broken vending machine. 

Once unlocked, this is where you can make your daily purchases for a variety of items. Some items are one-time only and appear as you need them, and others will refresh every 24 hours at the daily server reset. Some items also rotate; these items have sub-headings below with their full rotation.

1. Small and Large Soul
One type of 1h Soul and 8h Soul is sold each day in the vending machine, rotating between the three different Soul types: Order (Yellow), Neutral (Green), and Chaos (Red). In that order, the souls are: Splashturtle, Venustoad, and Charisnake.
