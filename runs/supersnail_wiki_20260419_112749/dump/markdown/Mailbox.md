1. The Mailbox
The Mailbox is where you will correspond with a variety of individuals throughout the course of the game:

Every day a Hitman will arrive, sent by the Demon Gods to defeat you.
<br>
Every day an Agent will arrive, sent by the Bureau to inquire about a Fugitive on the run.
<br>
Sometimes Visitors will appear to peddle their wares, or ask for feedback on something...
 Archaeologist, Clown Express, INTEL Dealer, Insurance Agent, Salesman, Peter Jr., and Indigent Nobleman

When finding Partners, the mailbox is how you will build a friendship and get to know them.
 Advisor Zhuge, Big Guy Park, Chichikaka, Demon Chan-hee, Ghost Zadako, Jiro Toyato, Kim Chi-yum, Lee Gi-na, Leeku, Peter Jr. (also a Visitor!), Prince Nerd's Soul, Red Doe, Sewer Turtle, Sleuth Doom, Taoist Yu Ji
 

Other notables visitors and invaders include that can appear at Home Base include:
 Rogue Survivor, aka Hitman No.9, the Sewer Turtle, Unicon, Dracula, and Grandpire.

Besides these, there's plenty of other possible encounters and fights you'll find during the game. Below are the storylines that appear throughout the game. All are listed alphabetically and are under the header of whoever you are mailing back and forth with (the exception to this are the Ishikawas, listed under "The Ishikawa Clan"). Be wary of spoilers! If you lose the fight, don't lose hope! They will reappear in the second tab, and stay there until you can win!
