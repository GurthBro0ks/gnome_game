1. Gene Simulation
Gene Simulation Trials can be done in the Computer.

1. General
The General Gene Simulation is an infinitely repeating simulation that can be attempted with any of the 6 forms. It gradually increases in power, and each level awards Travel Speedup +10m. in Addition, every 5th level awards B-tads, Reagents, and Evolution Chests.

1. Special
The Special Gene Simulation is a split between Form Genes and Special Genes. Form Genes are obtained during Gene Research and are necessary for upgrading Form Tiers. Special Genes are obtained from a variety of places, listed below. 

All of these can only be attempted by a certain Form, have a limited number of floors, and a variety of specific rewards each.

1. Gene Value Packs
Gene Value Packs can be purchased after certain milestones are met.
