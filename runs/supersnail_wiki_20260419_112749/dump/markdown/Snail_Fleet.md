1. Snail Fleet
The Snail Fleet is the unique element of Britland and is unlocked once the Britland INTEL tile 9 "Royal Navy". 

The Snail Fleet can consist of 5 ships at a time, and in order to make a ship you must have the blueprint for that ship. These are obtained from various sources.

Ships have 6 stats: Load, Armor, Cannon, Carronade, Sailor Limit, and Fleet Power. Sailor Limit is equal to the HP of a warship and Fleet Power determines the amount of DMG done at the start of a battle. Armor is warship DEF, Cannon is warship ATK, and Carronade is warship RUSH.

1. Warships
Warships are broken up into 3 types: Light, Medium, and Heavy and are obtained from multiple sources. Green and blue rank warships are all light, purple rank warships are all medium, and orange rank warships are all heavy.
