1. Alchemy Furnace
The Alchemy Furnace is near the Mailbox and is unlocked once Kim Chi-yum reaches 2 hearts. Here you will use Wish Coins to earn Relics and Scrolls. You can pick between any of the five AFFCTs to wish for that specific type, and wish with 1 or 10 coins at a time. Here you can also craft reagent, coating, fungi, and relic fragments.

1. Wishing Value Packs
Wishing Value Packs can be purchased after certain milestones are met.

1. Crafting

Crafting is unlocked once Toad Mercenary has been defeated and joins the pond. At first only alchemy reagent and fragment crafting is available at the green and red toad respectively. Further crafting can be unlocked at the green frog as follows:
- Coating crafting is unlocked once shell upgrades are unlocked with the **I need shell service!** Divine Dragon wish at the Altar.
- Fungi crafting is unlocked once the  is purchased in the **1.5M Total Power** Power Value Pack.
- Species Sequence crafting is unlocked once the  is purchased in the **Species Map Total Score 30** Traveler Map Value Pack.
- Organ Slime Crafting is unlocked once the  is purchased in the **Organ Score (10k)** Organ Value Pack.

1. General Crafting
Reagents, coatings, and species sequences can all be crafted in a similar manner. Each tier of an item (other than orange) can be crafted into a higher tier, with 10 of a lower tier equaling one of its higher tier. i.e. 10 green = 1 blue, 10 blue = 1 purple, and 10 purple = 1 orange. 

1. Fungi
Once unlocked, fungi can be crafted into a random higher tier. 5 green fungi = 1 random blue, 5 blue = 1 random purple, 5 purple = 1 random orange. 

1. Fragment Crafting
Once one of 5 AFFCTs has been selected, fragment crafting becomes available. Any spare fragments (from relics that have been fully upgraded) can be converted into a random, higher tier relic of that same AFFCT. 1. Wishing
Wishing uses Wish Coins, which can be obtained from the Vending Machine (600 W-Tads), Museum (135 museum medals), and ocassionally from the Fissure. They're also obtained by participating in Weekly Events. The <span style="color:#ffb066;">Taurus</span> relic increases the number of available Wish Coins in the Museum shop.

1. Chances
The rewards earned are completely RNG (random number generator) based, and can't be improved or manipulated. However, only relics, scrolls, and taichi fish are earned from wishing. 

There is a 61% chance of pulling a blue pearl, and within this a 60.6% chance for it to be a relic. 

There is a 27% chance of pulling a purple pearl, and within this a 9.1% chance for it to be a relic and a 18.2% chance for it to be an expert scroll. 

There is a 12% chance of pulling an orange pearl, and within this a 9.1% chance for it to be a scroll for that AFFCT and different chances for a variety of specific relics within that AFFCT. 

1. Weekly Wishing Event
Wishing week is one of the three Weekly Events. During this week, the more you wish, the more you get! While the general structure of each Wishing Event is the same, the rewards vary from week to week. Specifically, different Orange Relics that can be purchased during the event. 

Wish Packs can be received during Wishing Week that can reward Wish Relic Album fragments.

-*This information is accurate for the week of:** Sept 29 - Oct 5

1. Suggested Strategies
- Hoard all wish coins until Wishing Event to maximize rewards. It's recommended to have between 160 and 190 wish coins saved up for the week to reach the 3k tier rewards. 
- The event also rewards for spending taichi fish on fragment crafting, so it's not a bad idea to save all your crafting for this event as well. 

1. Wish Magic
1 Wish = 1 Point. There are 5 rounds total. Each round requires 80 Wish Coins and gives 1,100 Mystery Ash plus other rewards. Completing all five rounds will require 400 Wish Coins and awards 5,500 Mystery Ash.

1. How Many Mystery Ashes Do I Need?
1. 3,000 Mystery Ash Reward
To get the **3,000 Mystery Ash Reward** you will need a total of **6,000 Mystery Ashes** to buy the previous rewards. The least amount of Wish Coins you will need to do this is **180 Wish Coins**.
- **Complete all of the supplemental rewards:** 2,800 Mystery Ashes
- **Purchase all 7 Offering Bonuses:** 700 Mystery Ashes
- **Purchase 100 W-Tad Event Pack:** 100 Mystery Ashes
- **Complete Two Rounds (80 Wish Coins each):** 2,200 Mystery Ashes
- **20 additional Wish Coins:** 240 Mystery Ashes
- **Total:** 6,040 Mystery Ash

1. Two 3,000 Mystery Ash Rewards
To get two **3,000 Mystery Ash Rewards** you will need a total of **9,000 Mystery Ashes.** This can be achieved by completing all five rounds in the Wish Magic and acquiring all other sources of Mystery Ashes. Completing five rounds of Wish Magic requires **400 Wish Coins.**
- **Complete all of the supplemental rewards:** 2,800 Mystery Ashes
- **Purchase all Seven Offering Bonuses:** 700 Mystery Ashes
- **Complete all Five Rounds (80 Wish Coins each):** 5,500 Mystery Ashes
- **Total:** 9,000 Mystery Ashes [Optionally you can also purchase the 100 W-Tad Event Pack for an additional 100 Mystery Ashes.]

1. 5,000 Mystery Ash Reward
Currently it is not possible to get the **5,000 Mystery Ash Reward** without spending real money. The most you can earn without spending money is **9,000 Mystery Ashes** which will allow you to buy two **3,000 Mystery Ash Rewards**
