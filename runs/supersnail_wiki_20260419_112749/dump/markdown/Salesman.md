The Salesman is a Visitor that shows up to offer a random commodity. Occasionally, if you've been purchasing items from him, he will offer a survey. Rating him 0-stars 3 times and winning the fight rewards one random item from his list of commodities, as well as  (Soul of Salesman). Or he can show up from a positive review and give you  (repeatable). Eventually he will show up again to offer new items.
