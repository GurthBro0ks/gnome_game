1. Getting Stronger
<span style="color:#0000FF">After the tutorial, collect these Relics at Home:</span><br>
1. Mutant Fungus in the Fungus Farm
1. Gold Toilet Paper on the Fridge
1. Golden Finger at the entrance to the Garage
1. Talk to the Robot Guard to get the Nuclear Fusion Battery

<span style="color:#0000FF">Recommended evolution order:</span><br>
Organs>ATK>DEF>RUSH>HP>Others<br>You can unlock Gene Form Evolution after reaching max level!<br><span style="color:#8c8c8c ">(Upgrade Combat Stats first to make the game easier)</span>

<span style="color:#0000FF">Snail Form Choice:</span><br>
1. Demon Form has high ATK and DEF and can collect souls
1. Always use Dragon Form after you obtain it as it has the highest stats
1. Use Mutant Form for auto-play to collect Time Chests

<span style="color:#0000FF">Tips for embedding Relics:</span><br>
1. Recommended order: Compass>Home Facility>Biozilla>Museum
1. Make 2 Compass presets: one for battle and the other for exploring
1. Pay attention to Embed Perks<br>
<span style="color:#8c8c8c ">(T3+ Relics have them)</span>

<span style="color:#0000FF">Techniques for Exploration:</span>
1. Generally, the first page of the INTEL Tree boosts resource gain while the second page boosts stats
1. Prioritize perks related to areas. The further you explore, the more you get
1. Redeem perks related to combat stats before fighting Apostles
1. You don't have to dominate the Apostles. Exploration is more important.<br>
<span style="color:#8c8c8c ">(If you can't dominate an Apostle, consider exploring the next Realm for buffs)</span>

<span style="color:#0000FF">Tips for Stats Increase:</span>
1. Exploration Speed>Offering Speed>Research Speed>Refinement Speed
1. HARD stats (HP, ATK, RUSH, DEF)>minion stats>AFFCT stats (ART, FTH, FAME, CIV, TECH)
1. Before entering Murika, equally boost HARD stats and minion stats
1. Partners: upgrade Taoraemon and Prince Nerd's Soul first (Nick Note: Chichikaka and Leeku have Gene Sims at lvl. 10)
1. For minions, also increase minion number cap

<span style="color:#0000FF">Avatar can also make you stronger:</span>
1. Apostle Avatar: increases exploration stats
1. Civil Mirage Avatar: increases earnings at Home
1. Special Event Avatar: increases Research SPD
1. All Avatar stats work permanently once you obtain them

<span style="color:#0000FF">Join a club and participate in wars:</span>
1. Join a club and build a portal ASAP
1. Get a large number of Research Manuals from Club Shop
1. Get tons of resources from Card Shop and Traitor Shop
1. Complete multi-snail incidents to get more cards
1. Use Silver Tokens to defeat high-level legions and get currencies
1. Buy Random Species Sequence first in Traitor Shop
1. Complete Biozilla to buy Gene Essence from Biozilla Shop

<span style="color:#0000FF">How to increase Leadership:</span>
1. Prioritize Gene Research, such as Zombie Form T4
1. Collect Relics that increase Leadership, such as the Trojan Horse
1. Consume a certain number of Orange Fungi
1. Right-side Relic Talents can boost Leadership
1. Upgrade Minion Gear after clearing the Murikan Domain

<span style="color:#0000FF">Enhance your troop:</span>
1. Upgrade form tier to unlock stronger minions
1. Increase Leadership to deploy minions in battle
1. Repair the Rocket and build rocket devices to increase minion stats
1. Organize minions into 10-minion groups to gain stats bonuses
1. Collect and Upgrade Relics that buff your minions

<span style="color:#0000FF">Once enemy weakness is identified, proc RUSH:</span>
1. Tap on bubble above target to check weakness
1. Once RUSH procs, deal RUSH DMG each round
1. RUSH also procs CRIT and ELMT DMG

<span style="color:#0000FF">About DEF:</span>
1. Generally, DMG=ATK-DEF
1. DEF also reduces RUSH DMG
1. ELMT DMG ignores DEF but not ELMT RES

<span style="color:#0000FF">About CRIT:</span>
1. CRIT deals 150% DMG of normal attacks
1. CRIT DMG increases DMG done by CRITs
1. CRIT DMG REDUC lowers CRIT DMG takes

<span style="color:#0000FF">Other Effects:</span>
1. Lifesteal restores HP when dealing ATK DMG and CRIT DMG, but not ELMT DMG
1. Dodge prevents you from taking any DMG
1. ELMT DMG adds DMG to normal ATK and RUSH
1. Paralyzed enemies cannot move for a round

<span style="color:#0000FF">About Minions:</span>
1. Troop Battle applies the same rules as that of a Snail Battle
1. Leadership decides the number of minions you can deploy
1. The minions you deploy decide the stats of your troop
1. Minions may get wounded after battles
1. Wounded minions cannot be sent to anything
1. You can treat wounded minions to dispel the injury status
1. The number of wounded minions is decided by their lost HP and the casualty rate
1. Minions with low Power will take the HP loss first
<span style="color:#8c8c8c ">(Puppet minions can take damage for minions of the same tier)</span>

<span style="color:#0000FF">About Casualty Rate:</span>
1. In Troop battles, (DMG taken) x (casualty rate)=(Lost HP)
1. When your troop takes damage, minions with low Power will lose HP first
1. When HP - lost HP =0, the minions will become wounded
1. The casualty rates in different Domains can be reduced by increasing the ratings of their corresponding Museums
1. The casualty rate in Species War can be reduced through Card Exchange
1. The casualty rates in different Domains, Species Wars, and Species Histories are different; always check when you edit your troops

<span style="color:#0000FF">About Upgrading Minions:</span>
1. Zombie minions have relatively high HP and ATK
1. Demon minions have relatively high ATK and RUSH
1. Angel minions have relatively high HP and RUSH
1. Mutant minions have relatively high ATK and RUSH
1. Mecha minions have extremely high RUSH
1. Dragon minions have comprehensively high stats
1. All minions except Dragon ones can be converted into each other by spending W-Tadpoles in Minion Conversion Event
