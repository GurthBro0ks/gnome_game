=Apostles=
Once an apostle is beaten in combat for the first time, they are considered defeated. Once an apostle has been defeated in 3 rounds or less, they are considered "Dominated" and will no longer have to be fought on subsequent explorations. Each Area has an apostle and occasionally an apostle will have minions who must be defeated first. 

Similar to the Apostles in Kemet, Apostles of this Realm are broken up into different areas that can only be explored one at a time. Parsia has a secondary location called Sky City, with a different set of apostles. Below are all the Apostles for each area of Sky City.

 Note that **all** apostles and minions have the special stats of: *CRIT %, Lifesteal Rate %, Chance to trigger Stonehide %, Chance to cause Bleed %, Chance of Weakening Enemy %, Chance of Breaking Enemy %, CRIT RES %, Bleed RES %, Stonehide RES %, Lifesteal RES %, Weaken RES %, Break RES %, HIT, and Paralysis RES %*.
